information and rules for the game

🔄 Rules Rolling a 1 = lose current score and switch turn. Rolling 2–6 = add to current score. Clicking Hold = add current score to total and switch turn. Clicking New Game = reset all scores and start again.
▶️ How to Play Click Roll Dice to roll a number between 1 and 6. Click Hold to keep your current score and end your turn. Click New Game to restart the game at any time.
